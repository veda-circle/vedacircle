VEDA Circle is a 501(c) (3) non-profit organization founded with mission to promote Vedic Education Dissemination and Awareness (VEDA) for the benefit of our community. Its Tax ID is 27-3794048

  • Peace

    Promote world peace and harmony through Vedic lifestyle approaches such as yoga, meditation and prayers

  • Educate

    Educate, Disseminate and create awareness about the values of Vedas

  • Service

    Provide community service and conduct research in Vedas. Advancement of spirituality as described in Vedas

 VEDA Circle is prudently and sincerely run by efficient and able members who are elected to be part of the management committee. Our committee are a team of volunteers who strive for the upliftment of Hindu tradition and Vedic principles.
